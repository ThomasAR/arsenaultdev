Kenna Employee Directory

I was the Team Lead for the first-place team of the Kenna 2019 corporate hackathon. For our project, we developed an Atlassian/Confluence-integrated internal employee directory featuring the Microsoft Graph API to connect to the company's Outlook network. The app provides functionality to view all information about employees including calendar and seating information, as well as view and book meeting rooms.

A demo of this app will be available on my website soon.
